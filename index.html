Meet Our Teachers

"Guiding Lights of SKVP: A Message from Our Leaders" At Sri Kalinga Vidya Pitha, we believe that education is not just about books—it's about shaping lives. With the vision of our founder Satya Brata Samal, and the dedication of our principals Mr. Tapas Ranjan Pattnaik (Primary) and Mr. Kaushik Sir (High School), our team of passionate educators is committed to nurturing every child with care, discipline, and knowledge. We stand united in creating a vibrant, value-based learning environment where students grow into confident, capable individuals ready to lead tomorrow.

Our dedicated teachers are the heart of SKVP—mentors who go beyond the classroom to inspire curiosity, character, and creativity. Backed by smart classes, practical labs, and a green, disciplined campus, we ensure holistic development through academics, co-curricular activities, and cultural programs. From science exhibitions to art, music, and sports, every child is encouraged to explore their potential and shine. Together, we are building a nurturing space where tradition meets innovation, and every student feels seen, supported, and celebrated.
