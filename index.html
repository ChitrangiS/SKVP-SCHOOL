Our History

Established in 2013, Shree Kalinga Vidya Pitha has been a beacon of quality education in Umerkote.

We have successfully guided 7 batches of Class 10 students, producing numerous top scorers every year.

Our students have excelled in government scholarship exams like NRTS, securing bright futures.

+2 Science and Upasastri admissions are ongoing, with excellent results including a district topper.
